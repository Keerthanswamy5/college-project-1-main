Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program at PES Institute of Technology and Management, Shivamogga, is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in business management and administration. The three-year undergraduate program covers a wide range of subjects including marketing, finance, human resources, and organizational behavior. With a focus on practical learning and real-world applications, the BBA program at PESIAM Shivamogga prepares students for a successful career in the field of business. The curriculum is regularly updated to keep pace with the latest industry trends and practices, ensuring that students are well-prepared to meet the demands of the ever-evolving business world.

The BBA program at PESIAM Shivamogga is taught by a team of experienced faculty members who are experts in their respective fields. The faculty members are dedicated to providing students with a comprehensive understanding of business management concepts, as well as practical skills that are essential for success in the corporate world. In addition to classroom lectures, students also have the opportunity to participate in workshops, seminars, and industry visits, which help them gain real-world exposure and practical experience. The BBA program at PESIAM Shivamogga also includes internships and project work, which allow students to apply their knowledge and skills in a real-world setting and gain valuable industry experience.

Upon successful completion of the BBA program, students have a wide range of career opportunities available to them. Graduates of the program can pursue careers in marketing, finance, human resources, operations, and business development, among others. The strong foundation in business management and administration provided by the BBA program at PESIAM Shivamogga equips students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the field of business and make a meaningful contribution to the corporate world.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission to the BBA program at PES Institute of Technology and Management, Shivamogga, candidates must have completed their 10+2 examination from a recognized board with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. Admission to the BBA program is based on merit, and candidates are required to appear for an entrance examination conducted by the college.

Fees

The total fees for the BBA program at PES Institute of Technology and Management, Shivamogga, are INR 1,00,000 per year. The fees include tuition fees, examination fees, and other miscellaneous charges. Scholarships are available for meritorious students based on their academic performance and financial need.
