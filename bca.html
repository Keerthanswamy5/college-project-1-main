Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program at PES Institute of Technology and Management, Shivamogga, is designed to equip students with a strong foundation in computer applications and programming languages. The three-year undergraduate program covers a wide range of subjects including software engineering, database management, computer networks, and web technologies. With a focus on practical learning and hands-on experience, the BCA program at PESIAM Shivamogga prepares students for a successful career in the field of information technology. The curriculum is regularly updated to keep pace with the latest industry trends and technologies, ensuring that students are well-prepared to meet the demands of the ever-evolving IT sector.

The BCA program at PESIAM Shivamogga is taught by a team of experienced faculty members who are experts in their respective fields. The faculty members are dedicated to providing students with a comprehensive understanding of computer applications and programming concepts, as well as practical skills that are essential for success in the IT industry. In addition to classroom lectures, students also have the opportunity to participate in workshops, seminars, and industry visits, which help them gain real-world exposure and practical experience. The BCA program at PESIAM Shivamogga also includes internships and project work, which allow students to apply their knowledge and skills in a real-world setting and gain valuable industry experience.

Upon successful completion of the BCA program, students have a wide range of career opportunities available to them. Graduates of the program can pursue careers as software developers, web developers, database administrators, system analysts, and IT consultants, among others. The strong foundation in computer applications and programming languages provided by the BCA program at PESIAM Shivamogga equips students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the field of information technology and make a meaningful contribution to the industry.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission to the BCA program at PES Institute of Technology and Management, Shivamogga, candidates must have completed their 10+2 examination from a recognized board with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. Candidates must also have studied Mathematics as a compulsory subject at the 10+2 level. Admission to the BCA program is based on merit, and candidates are required to appear for an entrance examination conducted by the college.

Fees

The total fees for the BCA program at PES Institute of Technology and Management, Shivamogga, are INR 1,00,000 per year. The fees include tuition fees, examination fees, and other miscellaneous charges. Scholarships are available for meritorious students based on their academic performance and financial need.
